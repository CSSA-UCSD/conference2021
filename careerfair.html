    About

    The CSSA Career Fair provides company representatives a unique opportunity to interact and engage with UC San Diego's talented cognitive science students regarding career options, internships, and employment opportunities. This event is open exclusively to UC San Diego Cognitive Science students and alumni, and is coordinated by the Cognitive Science Student Association and faculty at UC San Diego.
